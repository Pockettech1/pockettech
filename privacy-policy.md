# Privacy Policy for PocketTech

**Last updated: April 17, 2026**

## Overview
PocketTech ("we," "us," or "our") is an HVAC diagnostic app developed by Ryan Smith. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address and name when you sign in with Apple

### Usage Data
- HVAC diagnostic queries submitted to our AI chat feature
- App usage patterns to improve performance

### Payment Information
- Subscription payments are processed by Apple. We do not store payment card information.

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide AI-powered HVAC diagnostic responses
- To manage your Pro subscription
- To improve app features and performance

## Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using Supabase. AI diagnostic queries are processed via Anthropic's Claude API and are not permanently stored beyond your session.

## Third-Party Services
- **Apple Sign In** – Authentication
- **RevenueCat** – Subscription management
- **Supabase** – Database and backend
- **Anthropic** – AI diagnostic responses

## Data Retention
We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy
PocketTech is not intended for users under 13 years of age.
